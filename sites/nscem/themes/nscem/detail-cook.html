Baked Lemon Honey Yogurt Chicken Drumettes

Ingredients

Serves 6 persons

  • 1 tablespoon Lemon Zest
  • 2 tablespoon Lemon juice
  • 3 cloves garlic, crushed and chopped finely
  • 2 tablespoons Honey
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 12 pieces chicken drumettes
  • 1 teaspoon mixed dried herbs
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons NESTLE Natural Set Yogurt
  • 1 MAGGI Chicken Stock Cube, crumbled

Directions

  • 1
  • 2
  • 3
  • 4
  1. Marinate chicken drumettes with MAGGI Chicken Stock Cube, lemon zest, yogurt, garlic, mixed herbs, lemon juice, salt and 1 tablespoon of honey for 2 hours.
  2. Pre-heat oven to 200 C. Line a baking tray with aluminium foil..
  3. Place chicken drumettes on the foil, brush with remaining honey, olive oil and bake for 20 minutes until cooked and lightly browned.
  4. Serve warm with lemon wedges and mayonnaise if desired..
